Explore Our Exciting Events

Discover the incredible activities and competitions we proudly host each year!

AI HACKATHON

Innovate, Compete, Dominate! Join a high energy AI competition where innovators tackle real-world challenges, collaborate and build groundbreaking solutions. Whether you're a beginner or a pro, learn from expert mentors and compete in cutting-edge challenges. Think fast, code smart and push boundaries in this ultimate problem-solving backgroung.
Ready to shape the future? Prove it!

WEB DEV

Web Dev is aimed at simulating real-world scenarios, this competition is designed as an intensive 6-hour competition where participants develop web-based solutions to practical problems. The competition can adapt based on whether a problem is internally generated or provided by a partnering company.

GENAI MASTERPIECE

AI Art Challenge: Unleash your creativity with Generative AI! Experiment with AI-powered tools to craft stunning digital art and redefine artistic expression. Whether you are an artist or AI enthusiast, explore limitless possibilities and showcase the future of AI-driven creativity!

EDATHON

Step into the world of Data Science with this hands-on, knowledge-packed event! Topics include: Data Cleaning and Preprocessing, Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA), Feature Engineering, Model Fine-Tuning and Business Impact and Decision Making. Whether you're a beginner or experienced enthusiast, this event will sharpen your skills and take your data expertise to next level. Join us and become a data science pro!
