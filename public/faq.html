What is NASCON?
NASCON (National Solutions Convention) is FAST-NU's flagship annual event featuring a wide range of competitions across domains like coding, gaming, singing, cooking, and more. Students from universities across Pakistan participate in this exciting three-day event.
Where is NASCON held?
NASCON is held entirely within the premises of FAST-NU. All competitions, activities, and the closing ceremony take place inside the university
Who can participate in NASCON?
Students from universities and colleges across Pakistan are eligible to register and compete in the various events at NASCON.
Is accomodation available for participants?
Yes, participants who require accommodation can avail of it. Accommodation arrangements are managed by the NASCON team and are located within the university facilities.
How I can register for events?
You can register for your desired events directly through the NASCON management app or the official NASCON website. Each event will have its own registration form and requirements with each event having its own separate registration charges.
Can I participate in multiple events?
Yes! You are welcome to participate in multiple events as long as their schedules do not clash. Make sure to check event timings before registering.
How can I get updates during NASCON?
All important updates, schedules, and announcements will be available through the NASCON management app, websites or social media accounts at diffrent platforms and at designated help desks inside the university.
