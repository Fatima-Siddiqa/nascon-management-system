Explore Our Exciting Events

Discover the incredible activities and competitions we proudly host each year!

NASCON GOT TALENT

Nascon got talent is a dynamic talent showcase inspired by the global Got Talent series, designed for students across Pakistan. This event provides a platform for singers, dancers, comedians, poets, magicians, and more to showcase their skills and compete for the title of the most talented peformer.

BAIT BAAZI

The Ultimate Poetry Duel!
Team recites Urdu verses, following a given harf, testing knowledge and pronunciation. A blend of tradition and competition -- who will claim victory?

DISH IT OUT

The Ultimate Cooking Showdown!
Get the dish name at 8 AM, plan within budget, and cook with a creative twist. Advance to round 2 to tackle a mystery ingredient. Taste, presentation and innovation decides the champion!

BATTLE OF THE BANDS

Where Music Takes Over!
NASCON'S most electrifying showdown returns! Top Local and University bands compete for glory in a high-energy battle of talent and passion. Get ready for unforgettable performances and an epic musical experience!
